Process

HOW HPZ
WORKS

A four-step system that adapts to you — not the other way around.

01
Build Your Profile

Complete the smart onboarding — goal, sport, level, equipment, season, and limitations.

02
Get Your Routine

HPZ selects the right routine from a full library of sport-specific programs based on your exact profile.

03
Train with Structure

Follow guided blocks — circuits, supersets, giant sets — with timers, sets, and cues.

04
Track Your Progress

Sessions save automatically. See your streak, total hours, history, and performance over time.

Features

EVERYTHING
YOU NEED.

HPZ is built with the tools a serious athlete actually needs — not watered-down fitness content.

Adaptive Routine Engine

The system selects your workout based on sport, level, equipment, goal, and season. No two athletes get the same routine.

Exercise Library — 136 Movements

Every exercise categorized by type, muscle group, equipment, and difficulty. Search and filter instantly.

Workout Engine

Block-based training player with real-time timers, set tracking, rest periods, and RPE logging.

Routine Builder

Create fully custom routines with your own blocks, exercises, sets and reps. Activate any routine to override the automated system.

Progress Tracking

Real session data — total sessions, streak, hours trained, weekly calendar, and training history all connected to your account.

In-App Notifications

Stay informed with training reminders, new content updates, and performance milestones delivered directly in the app.
